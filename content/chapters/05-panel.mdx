Every hex pane resists roughly **1,332 kN** at one atmosphere.
Pentagons carry about **883 kN**. The v3 dual-path joint separates
structural load from pressure seal so redundancy is intentional, not
incidental.

Seven layers stack from the vacuum side inward — sacrificial pane,
structural frame, transparent photovoltaic, electrochromic tint, gas
gap, deployable shade, and crew-facing inner frame.

Hardware language: 6061-T6 aluminum, NAS9306C-06 lockbolts on a
defined pitch, dual fluorosilicone gaskets with a tertiary RTV
channel for breach mitigation.
