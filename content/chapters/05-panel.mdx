Every hex pane resists roughly **1,332 kN** at one atmosphere. Pentagons
carry about **883 kN**. The v3 dual-path joint separates structural
load from pressure seal so redundancy is intentional, not incidental.

### Seven layers, vacuum to crew

The panel is an engineered stack — **140 mm** total depth — with every
layer doing a specific job. Scroll has pulled them apart vertically so
you can read each one in isolation.

| # | Layer | Function |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sacrificial debris pane | MMOD first-impact; replaceable |
| 2 | Outer structural frame | 6061-T6 aluminium, load path |
| 3 | Transparent photovoltaic | Power generation through the window |
| 4 | Electrochromic tint | Software-controlled opacity |
| 5 | Insulating gas gap | Thermal decoupling |
| 6 | Sliding opaque solar | Deployable shade / power boost |
| 7 | Inner structural frame | Crew-facing, pressure boundary |

### Joint hardware language

Every 2.25 m edge carries the same sealed interface — so a panel swap
in orbit doesn't need a bespoke gasket or fastener stock.

- **Frame:** 6061-T6 aluminium, **140 mm** total depth
- **Lockbolts:** NAS9306C-06 on Ø **4.83 mm** holes
- **Fastener coupon basis:** 14 fasteners over 550 mm at ~**39 mm pitch**
- **Primary seal:** dual flat fluorosilicone gaskets, **1.6 mm** thick,
  **10 mm** wide, **14 mm** centreline spacing, **25–35 %** compression
  target
- **Tertiary breach seal:** RTV in a semi-circular recess, **3 mm**
  radius × **3 mm** depth, **8 mm** inboard of inner-gasket centreline
- **Wiring conduit:** **10 mm × 10 mm**

Numbers above are locked in [CANONICAL.md](/#/canonical) §Joint /
Section Values. Any render or blueprint that disagrees is the thing
that needs fixing — not the number.

### Why seven layers, not five

Two extra layers look redundant on paper. They aren't:

1. **Layer 1 exists because layer 2 cannot be consumable.** MMOD risk
   demands replacement without touching structure.
2. **Layer 4 exists because layer 6 cannot be instant.** Sliding shade
   takes seconds. Electrochromic tint takes milliseconds.

Remove either and the panel only works in one failure mode.
