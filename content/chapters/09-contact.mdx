HCSA is a research effort at Brown University. Faculty advisors:
**Dr. Rick Fleeter** and **Dr. Marco Cross**. Project lead:
**Manos Theofanous**.

Ways to help:

- **Funding.** Prototype materials, test articles, and student
  research time — especially for joint coupons and glazing risk
  reduction.
- **Fabrication.** Precision machining, seal testing, and metrology
  partners accelerate the path from CAD to trusted hardware.
- **Technical review.** Aerospace structures, ECLSS, and
  human-factors experts who can stress the assumptions help the
  project mature.
