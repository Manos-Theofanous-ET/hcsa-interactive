HCSA is a research effort at **Brown University**.

Faculty advisors: **Dr. Rick Fleeter** and **Dr. Marco Cross**. Project
lead: **Manos Theofanous**.

### Where help moves the project fastest

| You have | We can use it for | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Funding** | Joint coupons, glazing risk reduction, student research time | P5 is the bottleneck to structural validation |
| **Precision fabrication** | Frame milling, lockbolt hole to NAS9306C-06 | One validated joint unlocks all 32 panels |
| **Seal + metrology** | Fluorosilicone compression testing, RTV recess inspection | Seal performance is currently modelled, not measured |
| **Aerospace structures review** | FEA + physical correlation on v3 joint | Honest validation loop |
| **ECLSS expertise** | Hybrid bio + physico-chemical closure | Closure is provisional; reviewers tighten the model |
| **Human factors** | Interior-zone layout, observation + exercise volumes | The perimeter is a UX problem too |

### How to get involved

Research partners, sponsorship discussions, and technical review all
flow through Brown University channels. If the geometry, the seal
stack, or the staged validation plan surfaced something you want to
push back on — we want the pushback. Design decisions are still
changeable at the levels P5 → P7 touch.
